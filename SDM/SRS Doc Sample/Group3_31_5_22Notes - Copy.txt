	Requirement Specification: 
		Introduction: to provide the complete solution for stakeholders as well as customer through a single gateway using the internet .it also describe the functionality the product need to fulfill all stakeholders needs.
		             scope:
                                   the system allows the customer to maintain their cart for adding and removing the product over internet.
                Objective:  By sitting at any corner of the country you can avail the services at your place , 
                            can be achieved by using this application.
                          
                stakeholders:
                             customer
                             salers
                             destributors(logistics)
                             vendors
	
	        Functional Requirement :

			Product Catalog: product based on top brands ,amount range, product size, product discount ,quality 
		
			Shopping Cart: after login or registration can placed the order or cancle the order of product from shopping cart.
			
                        Payment Processing: 
                                various payment options :
                                                         cod
                                                         emi
                                                         credit cards
                                                         debit cards
                                                         upi     
                        
                        Billing: 
                                linking of the payment gateways with the bank servers 
                                provided with discount coupons ,delivery charges and gst will included while billing.
			
                        Wishlist: customer willing to buy product listed and can save in the list, whenever you logged in you get the list in your application.  
			
                        Delivery(Shipping): system will show the status of product shippment to customer as well as delivery partner .
                                            customer have authority to cancle the order while shippment.
                                            delivery person should get the number of delivery of day at a time.

			OrderProcessing: placing the order ,
                                         selecting the product and its quantity as per user requirements.
                                         if order place sucessfully product will packed and dispatch.
                                         after ordering product system will send one copy of bill to customer via email and another one for system database.
                                         if not statisfied with product it can be returned with 100% refund 

			CRM:  feedback from the customer and timely notification of offers to customer.
                              seggregation customer on the basis of premium membership.
                              fullfill the customer satisfaction increase the performance of company.

			BI: seasonal product sale information to help to set future growth of business.
                            provide the related option based product purchases or past purchase order.

			Recommendation: best selling product and trending products on site will recomended to the customer.
                                        

			Reviews: rating options for product as well as delivery person.
			
                        Profile: profile of customer , vender,  salers, delivery persons.
